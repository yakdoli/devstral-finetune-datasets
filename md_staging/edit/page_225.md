## 4.10 Status Bar

Essential Edit provides support to include a built-in Status Bar at the bottom of the control with different panels displaying different information. The built-in panels are as follows.

- TextPanel
- StatusPanel
- EncodingPanel
- FileNamePanel
- CoordsPanel
- InsertPanel
