### WinForms-specific conventions
- **ScheduleControl**: The control used to display the calendar and manage schedule views.
- **ScheduleGrid**: The area where the schedule data is displayed in a grid format.
- **ContextMenu**: A right-click menu that appears when interacting with the ScheduleGrid.

## Instructions to Change Schedule View
1. To change the **Month view to a Day view**:
   - **Right-click** the **ScheduleGrid area** of the **ScheduleControl** to display a **ContextMenu**.
   - From the ContextMenu, **select "Day"**.
