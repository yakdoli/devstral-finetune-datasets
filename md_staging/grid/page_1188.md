## 4.14.1 Localization Support for ComboBox Cell

### Overview

- Localization support allows customization of ComboBox cell content based on language or cultural preferences.
- Provides the ability to localize using the `ConvertToString` method of the `Status` class.

### Content

#### Localization Support Overview

Localization support allows you to set the content in a ComboBox cell according to the language or culture of a specific country or region. You can localize the content in the ComboBox cell using the `ConvertToString` method of the `Status` class.

##### Use Case Scenarios

With this feature, you can localize the options in the ComboBox cell to display the content according to the language or culture of a specific country or region.
