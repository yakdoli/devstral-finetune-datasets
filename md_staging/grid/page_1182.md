## Content

### 4.13 Support for Skin Manager in Windows Forms Grid
This feature enables you to easily apply themes to all grids in an application through a single functional call. The Skin Manager provides support for the following themes:

- Office 2007 Blue
- Office 2007 Black
- Office 2007 Silver
- Office 2010 Blue
- Office 2010 Black
- Office 2010 Silver

#### Use Case Scenarios
When you create an application with multiple Grid controls, you can apply uniform themes for the entire form and its child controls using this feature.
