# Essential Grid for Windows Forms

## Overview

The Portfolio Manager is a tool that allows you to track and manage your investment portfolio effectively. Key features include:

- **Dashboard**: Provides a consolidated view of your mutual fund holdings, including details such as rate of change per day, current market price, and total returns.
- **Performance Analyzer**: Offers in-depth analysis of stock performance.
- **Contribution Analyzer**: Helps assess how individual stocks contribute to your overall portfolio.
- Additional features for detailed stock management and views.

## Content

### Portfolio Manager

#### Overview

**Dashboard**:
- **Overview** - Provides a consolidated view of your mutual fund holdings. You can access useful information on your investments such as rate of change per day, current market price, total returns, etc. You have the option to add or delete desired stocks.
- **Detail View** - Provides a more detailed view, allowing you to get the stock details of the mutual funds.
- **Industry/Sector View** - Provides a consolidated view of your stock holdings.

#### Interface Details

The Portfolio Manager interface includes the following features:
- **Tabs**:
  - **Overview**
  - **DetailView**
  - **Industry/SectorView**
  - **StockExchangeView**
- **Add new stocks** section for adding stocks to the portfolio:
  - **Symbol Name**: Enter the stock symbol.
  - **Quantity**: Specify the quantity of the stock.
  - **Price**: Set the price or use the current market price.
  - **Account**: Select the account associated with the stock.
  - **StockExchange**: Choose the stock exchange.
