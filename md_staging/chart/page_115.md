# Essential Chart for Windows Forms

## Overview
- The Funnel chart is a single series chart representing data as portions of 100%.
- The Funnel chart does not use any axes.
- Funnel charts can be viewed in 2D or 3D mode.
- Useful for representing stages in a sales process and showing potential revenue for each stage.
- Identifies potential problem areas in an organization's sales processes.

## Content

### Funnel Charts
#### Description
Funnel charts are often used to represent stages in a sales process and show the amount of potential revenue for each stage. This type of chart can be useful also in identifying potential problem areas in an organization's sales processes. A funnel chart is similar to a stacked percent bar chart.
