#### Reko Chart with `ReversalAmount = "3"`

**Figure 186: Renko Chart with ReversalAmount = "3"**

A Renko Chart is depicted with varying heights of orange and blue blocks. The x-axis is labeled with dates: Jan/01, Jan/11, Jan/21, Jan/31, Feb/10, Feb/20, and Mar/01. The y-axis represents values ranging from 20 to 45.

- **Red/Orange Block:** Represents upward price movements.
- **Blue Block:** Represents downward price movements.
- **ReversalAmount = "3":** Indicates the value used to determine whether a new block is formed based on price reversal.
