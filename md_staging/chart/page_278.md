### 4.5.1.50 OptimizePiePointPositions

#### Overview

This section specifies whether the data points with smaller values are grouped together and ordered. By default, the points are ordered in the sequence they are added to the series.

#### Details

- **Possible Values**
  - **True** - Enables optimization of pie chart point positions.
  - **False** - Disables optimization of pie chart point positions.
