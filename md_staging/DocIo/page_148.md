## 4.4.1.2.4.2 DropDown

The WDropDownFormField class represents a drop-down form field in the Word document. To add a drop-down form field to the Word document, click DropDown Form Field on the Forms toolbar.

![Figure 50: Forms Panel](image.png)

![Figure 51: DropDown Form Field Properties](image.png)

The DropDownSelectedIndex property is used to define the index of the record to be displayed in the drop-down form field. The record is chosen among the collection of the drop-down records. This collection is accessible through the DropDownItems property.
