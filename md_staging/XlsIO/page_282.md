### PivotCharts

PivotCharts are interactive graphical representations of PivotTable data that allow rapid analysis of the displayed data. PivotTable is an Excel feature that helps in summarizing large volumes of data, such as stock market reports, Cash flow reports, etc. A PivotTable can aggregate data instead of analyzing rows upon large volumes of records, and with a few clicks, the PivotChart allows rapid, dynamic, and flexible data analysis. The following sections describe the creation of PivotTables and PivotCharts.
