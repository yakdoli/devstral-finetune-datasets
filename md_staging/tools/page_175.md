### 3.4.3.6.1 Dock Arrow Settings

#### Docking Process Guidance
- **DockingManager**: Provides guidance during the process of docking a control using the `DragProviderStyle` property.

#### Dock Arrow Visibility
- **VS2005 or Whidbey Style**: When dragging a docked control, arrows are displayed on all four sides of the form. These arrows indicate docking positions, and keeping the mouse pointed at an arrow displays a shadow-like appearance corresponding to the docking side.
