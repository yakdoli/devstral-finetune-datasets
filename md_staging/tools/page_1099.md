#### Menus Framework
The Menus Framework allows you to create Menus and toolbars using easy-to-use components:
- `MainFrameBarManager` and `ChildFrameBarManager`.
- The BarItems required on menus and toolbars can be dragged-and-dropped at any appropriate position.

#### XP Toolbars and CommandBars
- **XP Toolbars** provide a toolbar look and feel that can be dragged-and-dropped on a form.
- They can be docked or floated as needed.
- **CommandBars** can be dragged-and-dropped on the form to implement dockable/floatable menus and toolbars similar to Visual Studio .NET.

#### Office XP and Office 2007 Look and Feel
The Essential Tools Menus package includes the XP Menus framework, offering:
- The latest Office XP and Office 2007 look and feel for menus, toolbars, and context menus.
- Enhanced functionality and usability for users familiar with these interfaces.
