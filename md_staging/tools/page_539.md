### BorderStyles for ColorPickerUIAdv

#### Figure 327: BorderStyles for ColorPickerUIAdv

The image contains three different BorderStyles for the ColorPickerUIAdv control:

1. **Automatic**
   - Features both "Theme Colors" and "Standard Colors" with a variety of color options.
   - A "More Colors..." option is available.

2. **FixedSingle**
   - Similar to the "Automatic" style but with a slightly different color arrangement and spacing.

3. **Fixed3D**
   - Maintains the "Theme Colors" and "Standard Colors" but with a more condensed layout.
