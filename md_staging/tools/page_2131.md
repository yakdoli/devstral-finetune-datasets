### 3.19.3.2 Splitter Position

#### Overview
- The Splitter Position can be adjusted manually within the TabSplitterContainer control at design time or at runtime.
- To specify the position programmatically, use the `SplitterPosition` property.

#### Implementation

- **C#**
  ```csharp
  this.tabSplitterContainer1.SplitterPosition = 95;
  ```

- **VB.NET**
  ```vb
  Me.tabSplitterContainer1.SplitterPosition = 95
  ```
