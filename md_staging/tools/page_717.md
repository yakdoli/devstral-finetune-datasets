#### Creating DomainUpDownExt Control Programmatically

The `DomainUpDownExt` control can also be created programmatically. Here are the steps to do so:

1. Add the following assembly references:
   - `Shared.Base`
   - `Shared.Windows`
   - `Tools.Base`
   - `Tools.Windows`
   
   and include the required namespace.

```csharp
using Syncfusion.Windows.Forms.Tools;
```
