## Content

### 3.12.2.8 Super Accelerator

#### Super Accelerator Overview
SuperAccelerator is a component designed to accelerate the click event of items by using a single keystroke without the need for mouse hovering.

1. **Drag-and-drop the SuperAccelerator on your form.**

   ![SuperAccelerator in Toolbox](Figure 1279: SuperAccelerator in Toolbox)

#### Steps to Use SuperAccelerator
- **Step 1:** Drag-and-drop the SuperAccelerator component from the toolbox onto your form.

   **Figure 1279: SuperAccelerator in Toolbox**
   ![SuperAccelerator in Toolbox](https://i.imgur.com/a/Syncfusion/SuperAccelerator.png)

- **Step 2:** Once the SuperAccelerator component is added to a form, an extended property will be added to the properties of every item in the toolstrip or tab item in the RibbonControlAdv.

#### Key Features
- **Accelerated Click Event:** The SuperAccelerator allows items to trigger click events using a single key stroke.
- **Flexibility:** It can be integrated into forms containing toolstrip or tab items.

## API Reference
- **Namespace:** Syncfusion.Windows.Forms.Tools
- **Component:** SuperAccelerator
- **Properties:** Extended properties are added to items in toolstrip or tab items.
