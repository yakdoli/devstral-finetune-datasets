#### Figure 1034: Desktop Alignment Options

This figure illustrates the different desktop alignment options available, each labeled with its corresponding positioning:

- **LeftTop**: Alignment at the top-left corner.
- **RightTop**: Alignment at the top-right corner.
- **Center**: Central alignment on the desktop.
- **SystemTray**: Placement in the system tray area.
- **LeftBottom**: Alignment at the bottom-left corner.
- **RightBottom**: Alignment at the bottom-right corner.
