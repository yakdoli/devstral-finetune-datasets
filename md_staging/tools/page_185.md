## Content

### DockingClientPanel with a non-docked TabControlAdv Control

#### Figure 96: DockingClientPanel with a non-docked TabControlAdv Control

**Description:**
Use the **DockingClientPanel** control for hosting child controls on non-MDI forms that contain docking windows. The **DockingClientPanel** interacts with the **DockingManager** to provide containment bounds that are automatically resized when controls are docked/undocked within the host form.
