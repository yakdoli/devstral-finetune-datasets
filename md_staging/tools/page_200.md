### Description of Color Schemes

1. **Blue Scheme**
   - Default color scheme resembling the Office 2007 blue theme.
   
2. **Silver Scheme**
   - Neutral color scheme with silver accents, suitable for modern interfaces.
   
3. **Black Scheme**
   - Dark mode theme with a black background, offering high contrast for visibility.
   
4. **Custom Color Scheme**
   - Allows users to define their own color schemes for personalized styling.

### Usage Examples:

- **Blue Scheme**
  - Provides a professional look similar to Office 2007 applications.
  
- **Silver Scheme**
  - Ideal for users who prefer a sleek and minimalistic design.
  
- **Black Scheme**
  - Preferred for users who work in low-light environments or enjoy a modern, dark theme.
  
- **Custom Color Scheme**
  - Enables customization to match brand colors or personal preferences.
