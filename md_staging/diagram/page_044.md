## Essential Diagram for Windows Forms

### Overview
- Demonstrates how to use the `OverviewControl` in Syncfusion Windows Forms.
- Explores the addition of the `OverviewControl` to the designer for a perspective view of the diagram model.

## Content

### Step-by-Step Guide

1. Open the **Toolbox** in your Windows Forms project.
2. Locate and select the `OverviewControl` from the **Syncfusion Windows 4.0 vs2010 Toolbox**.
   - The `OverviewControl` provides a perspective view of the diagram model.
3. Drag the `OverviewControl` from the **Toolbox** and drop it onto the **Designer Form window**.

   **Figure 21: Dragging OverviewControl to the Designer**

   The Overview control will be added to the designer, and its dependent assemblies will be added to the project once you have dropped it to the Designer Form window.
